BANDS 

 

A rubber band is a flexible loop or ring made from rubber or a synthetic elastomer material. It is designed to stretch and return to its original shape when released. Rubber bands are commonly used for binding or holding items together, providing a temporary closure, or creating tension in various applications.

These bands are usually circular in shape and vary in size, thickness, and elasticity. They are commonly used in offices, homes, industries, and various other settings for tasks such as bundling papers, securing objects, organizing items, and more. Rubber bands come in different colors and materials to suit different needs, and they are known for their versatility, cost-effectiveness, and ease of use.
